# Source Pack: Company And Adversarial Intelligence

Load this file for company, product, customer, short-report, lawsuit, or
thesis-stress-testing work.

## 1. Best source classes

Combine:
- official company narrative
- customer, partner, or distribution evidence
- hiring and org-shape evidence
- product docs, demos, repos, and release cadence
- adversarial analysis such as short reports and lawsuits
- user and operator commentary

## 2. What to test

Check whether the story is internally consistent across:
- product claims
- customer evidence
- pricing or go-to-market behavior
- hiring patterns
- technical artifacts
- legal or reputational pressure

## 3. Handling adversarial sources

Use short reports, complaints, and lawsuits as structured attack paths:
- identify the concrete allegations
- separate factual allegations from rhetoric
- verify each allegation against other source classes
- record what remains unresolved instead of forcing closure
