# Due Diligence Research

Stress-test a story until the weak points are visible.

Read shared references:
- `../researcher/references/core-method.md`
- `../researcher/references/source-hierarchy.md`
- `../researcher/references/source-packs-company-intel.md`
- `../researcher/references/saturation-and-counterevidence.md`
- `../researcher/references/query-shaping.md`

## Workflow

1. Define the target thesis and the main diligence questions.
2. Collect the official narrative, then test it against behavioral evidence.
3. Pull customer, product, hiring, and artifact signals into the same ledger.
4. Run a mandatory adversarial branch with short reports, lawsuits, complaints,
   or skeptical reporting when available.
5. End with a risk register and a consistency judgment.

## Constraints

- Do not trust official narrative without reality checks.
- Do not treat adversarial material as automatic truth.
- Record unresolved allegations separately from verified ones.

## Output

Create or update `research-{topic}.md` while working so supporting evidence,
attack paths, and unresolved risks are visible.

Prefer sections like:
- Diligence Frame
- Official Narrative
- Evidence That Supports It
- Evidence That Challenges It
- Risk Register
- Bottom-Line Judgment
