# Operator Reality Research

Surface what people actually experience, not just what institutions say.

Read shared references:
- `../researcher/references/core-method.md`
- `../researcher/references/source-hierarchy.md`
- `../researcher/references/source-packs-field-signals.md`
- `../researcher/references/query-shaping.md`

## Workflow

1. Define whose lived experience matters: beginner, senior operator, manager,
   customer, or insider.
2. Gather signals from forums, social platforms, review sites, profiles, and
   repos.
3. Extract repeated patterns, hidden requirements, and day-to-day friction.
4. Cross-check community claims against official and behavioral evidence.
5. Separate broad patterns from one-off anecdotes.

## Constraints

- Never treat a single post as representative.
- Segment sources by role and seniority where possible.
- Pay special attention to repeated disagreement between public narrative and
  operator reality.

## Output

Create or update `research-{topic}.md` while working so repeated patterns,
frictions, and caveats are externalized.

Prefer sections like:
- Population Frame
- Repeated Patterns
- Hidden Frictions
- Mismatch With Public Narrative
- Confidence And Caveats
