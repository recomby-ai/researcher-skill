# Opportunity Research

Look for credible upside, not just loud narratives.

Read shared references:
- `../researcher/references/core-method.md`
- `../researcher/references/source-hierarchy.md`
- `../researcher/references/source-packs-jobs.md`
- `../researcher/references/source-packs-field-signals.md`
- `../researcher/references/source-packs-company-intel.md`

## Workflow

1. Define what counts as an opportunity in this context.
2. Map the space quickly and identify where momentum appears.
3. Test for real demand, hiring, distribution, adoption, or operator pull.
4. Challenge hype with contradiction paths and weak-signal checks.
5. Rank opportunities by evidence quality, timing, and accessibility.

## Constraints

- Do not confuse attention with opportunity.
- Require at least one behavioral or market signal before calling something
  promising.
- Separate upside from feasibility.

## Output

Create or update `research-{topic}.md` while working so ranked opportunities
and hype checks are visible.

Prefer sections like:
- Opportunity Frame
- Candidate Opportunities
- Evidence Of Demand
- Risks And Hype Checks
- Ranked Opportunities
- Recommended Next Bets
